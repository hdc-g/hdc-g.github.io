Lectures series on certain Trace Formulae

by Masao Tsuzuki (都築 正男)

Registration form

This is the website for the lectures series given by Masao Tsuzuki organized by Héctor del Castillo, Yeansu Kim and Andrei Seymour-Howell, together with the LFANT group.

The lectures will be held at the Storium, Chonnam National University, Gwangju, from April 27 to April 29.

Program (tentative)

  • Lecture I: Basics on automorphic forms on GL(2) and GL(3).
  • Lecture II: Introduction to harmonic analysis of GL(2) and GL(3) over real numbers.
  • Lecture III, IV:
      (i) The Selberg trace formula for compact arithmetic quotients of GL(3) and its application to the prime geodesic theorem.
      (ii) The invariant trace formula of GL(2).
  • Lecture V: Towards an explicit invariant trace formula of GL(3).
  • Background Lecture I: Introduction to automorphic forms on GL(2).
  • Background Lecture II: Automorphic representation of GL(2).
  • Background Lecture III: Abelian trace formula: Poisson summation.
